### Ingredients
- 300 g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 1 tsp salt

---
### Directions
#### Pasta
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
2. Reserve 125 ml pasta water and drain.

#### Lemon Sauce
1. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.
2. Add lemon juice and a splash of pasta water.
3. Toss through spaghetti and season with salt.
