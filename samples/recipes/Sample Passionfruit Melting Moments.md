### Ingredients
#### Biscuits
- 250 g flour
- 150 g butter, softened
- 70 g icing sugar

#### Passionfruit Butter Cream
- 40 g butter, softened
- 120 g icing sugar
- 1 tbsp passionfruit pulp
- 1 tsp lemon juice

---
### Directions
#### Biscuits
1. Cream butter and icing sugar.
2. Fold in flour to form a soft dough.
3. Bake at 180C for 12 minutes.

#### Passionfruit Butter Cream
1. Beat butter and icing sugar until smooth.
2. Mix in passionfruit pulp and lemon juice.
3. Sandwich cooled biscuits with filling.
